Q&A

The questions that come up most.
Why does the homepage only show one signal?
Every week stays public for one week so there's always one clear, current thing to look at — not a wall of old links to sort through. Once a new signal goes live, the old one moves to the members archive instead of disappearing for good.
What happens to past signals?
Every past signal is kept, indexed, and searchable in the members archive. If you've paid for access once, you keep it — nothing you've paid for ever gets taken away.
Do I need an account to get this week's signal?
No. The current week's signal is open to everyone — just enter your email to get it sent over. An account is only needed to browse past signals in the archive.
Is this a subscription?
Most signals are free, one at a time, no commitment. A separate paid membership unlocks the full archive plus any paid-only signals, and you can cancel anytime.
How do I know this isn't just more AI hype?
Fair question — the space moves fast and there's a lot of noise. Every signal is a real, usable tool, template, or explainer, not a prediction or a pitch. Try this week's free one and judge for yourself.
